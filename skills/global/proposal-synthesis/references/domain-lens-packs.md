# Domain Lens Packs

Choose the smallest pack that fits the task.
Do not mix packs unless the task is genuinely cross-domain.

## Research

Prioritize:
- novelty or contribution shape
- falsifiability
- evidence path
- baseline or comparison logic
- first discriminating experiment

Good proposal differences:
- new hypothesis versus stronger baseline-driven framing
- low-risk measurement plan versus ambitious method change
- data-centric versus model-centric path

## Development

Prioritize:
- user value
- implementation shape
- integration risk
- delivery cost
- operational or maintenance burden

Good proposal differences:
- narrow incremental path versus structural redesign
- local workaround versus platform investment
- faster delivery path versus stronger long-term boundary model

## General Problem-Solving

Prioritize:
- leverage
- reversibility
- stakeholder fit
- downside containment
- what can be tested first

Good proposal differences:
- faster reversible move versus higher-upside commitment
- centralization versus delegation
- process change versus tooling change

## Cross-Domain Rule

If the task spans research and development, keep one primary pack and import only one or two criteria from the secondary pack.
Do not turn the decision frame into a giant rubric.
